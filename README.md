# PwC (pwc)

PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) is one of the Big Four professional services firms providing audit and assurance, tax, and management consulting services globally. PwC operates as a network of member firms across approximately 150 countries with hundreds of thousands of professionals. PwC publishes thought-leadership content and operates client portals for engagement delivery and document exchange but does not publish a public developer API or open partner integration portal at the corporate level - client connectivity is bilateral and project-scoped.
